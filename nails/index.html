The Hundred Uses of Nails
- An Interactive children’s book

Mandarin Version

The Hundred Uses of Nails is a book that I wrote, designed, and illustrated intender for my daughter. The book is designed with push, pull, slide, rotate, and flap mechanisms perfect for young readers, ages 2-6 years old. With interactive system background, I combined fun play with educational purposes mainly promote toddlers’ personal hygiene issues. This book also could be used to develop younger children fine motor skills, at the same time think critically about body parts when comparing with other animals. A physical copy of the book is created for user testing amount 5 children, with great feedbacks. Sadly due to cost of printing, this boos is not published.

Process

The book is designed with push, pull, slide, rotate, and flap mechanisms perfect for young readers to discover on their own. Initially I draw out the storyboards, characters interface to try ideas on my daughter. Later created digital formats using both Illustrator.

Result

A physical copy of the book is created for user testing amount 5 children collected user feedback through interview, with great feedbacks. Sadly due to cost of printing, this boos is not published.
