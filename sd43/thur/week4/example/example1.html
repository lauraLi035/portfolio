To catch a Pokémon:

  1. When a wild Pokémon is nearby, your device will vibrate, and the Pokémon will appear on the map
  2. The Pokémon will appear in front of you
  3. Touch and hold your Poké Ball.
  4. A target ring will appear around the Pokémon.
