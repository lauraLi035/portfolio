Cinfluencer
-Graphic & Web Project

To Site

Cinfluencer is a start-up digital platform for internet celebrities by Sincubator , a Vancouver tech incubator. Cinfluencer is dedicated to providing internet celebrities and KOIs from all sectors of the internet with a hub to advertise, expand their digital footprint and secure opportunities for commercial work.

I kickstarted the project by building a mockup using Figma, which was approved by the supervisor. The next step was to build a mobile responsive website using HTML CSS by bootstrap. Cinfluencer is still at the start stage of the project, limited marketing assets needed. Including posters, a mobile responsive showcase website with just a landing page.

Graphic Design - Digital & Printable

In addition to completing the website, the project involved creating brand-new visual assets for Cinfluencer for marketing purposes. The assets needed to adapt to digital platforms include, but are not limited to, Wechat, Weibo, Twitter Facebook, Instagram, etc., as well as conventional printed media. I ended up producing various formats and versions of the visual assets for their respective use while ensuring a coherent look based on the branding and style guidelines.
