A child play

WitChase is a 2D puzzle mobile game designed for Android Tablet. The main play focuses on exporling the game world and combing items. The story begin when the main character, Eithne a witch going on a journey to defeat the evil villain through exploring and crafting items. By fulfilling the NPCs' requests.

Process

As we were a five member team working on a full term project. We had significant amount of discussions on iteration and refining ideas. We initially draw out the storyboards, characters interface to communicate ideas, laster we practice our idea into digital formats using both Photoshop and Illustrator finally built our game prototype with Unity2D.
{{sketchesSlide.text}}

My Role

I led story board development, gameplay iteration, user interface development and user experience refinement. I conducted user testing sessions and collected user feedback through interview, survey and questionnaire. Later I analyzed and reflected user feedback to refine for better usability and game experience.

Result

Throughout the process of the project, I developed my user researching and analytical skills which helped me to reflect on feedback to refine user experience and our team ended up being one of the top popular games in public voting during school showcase session.
