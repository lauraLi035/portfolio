light seeker

This project mimic the behavior of moths. Moths as a type of insect, they are attacked to light. So my beam bot is flying all the time and sensing light. As a result, when there is light, the leds of the bot will shine, vise versa, when there is minimal light, leds will dime eventually disappear. This behavior is to mimic the excitement moths seeking light.

Circuit diagram

In this project, 2 circuit was used to solve to power source issue. To ensure the proper performance of the motor, two battery is connected serially to produce 6V. The leds get 3V of power, and based on the light resistor it adjust according by dimming.
