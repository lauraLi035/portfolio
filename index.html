About Me

All products surround us are designed to communicate. A mug says “pick me up by the handle, and I would hold liquid for you.” I have great passion in designing how computer interfaces elements ‘talk’ to users. I believe good designed products should be modify themselves to meet users’ expectations and needs. Armed with software background, knowledge of human perception, and a heart of never giving up, I am on a journey to the ‘ideal’ solution. Therefore,my strong beliefs can assist in fulfilling users' expectations and needs.
